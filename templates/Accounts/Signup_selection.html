Our Mission
Every year, the Nepal government provides various benefits and grants to promote the agriculture sector. However, these grants rarely reach the farmers who actually need them. One of the main reasons for this is the lack of proper dissemination of information.
We, the Krishi Jankari team, aim to bridge this gap by providing comprehensive information about every grant available in farmers' localities. Our mission is to ensure that farmers are well-informed about the opportunities available to them, thus enabling them to take full advantage of the benefits provided by the government. We will also assist them in doing paperwork required for these grants by taking minimal charges.
